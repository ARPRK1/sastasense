Questions, answered

Is SastaSense free?

Yes. Searching, price history, and price-drop alerts are all free. There are no ads and no account needed to search.

Which stores do you compare?

Amazon.in, Flipkart, Croma and Reliance Digital today — with more on the way. You always see which store has the best price highlighted.

How do price-drop alerts work?

Open any product, set your target price (add as many as you like), and we watch it in the background. When it hits your price, your alert fires — and can email you too.

Why do some results say “demo”?

When a store temporarily blocks automated requests, we show clearly-labelled sample data so the page never breaks. Real listings always take priority over sample ones.

How accurate are the prices?

Every price is stamped with when it was fetched, so you always know how fresh it is. Click through to confirm before buying — prices can change any minute.
